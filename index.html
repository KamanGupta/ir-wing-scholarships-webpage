Scholarships for International Exposure

DAAD Scholarship

Description: Supports study or research in Germany for academically strong UG/PG students.

Eligibility: Indian students in UG/PG programs with good academic record.

Stipend: €861/month + travel allowance + health insurance.

Visit Official Website

MITACS Globalink Research Internship

Description: A 12-week summer research internship in Canada in fields like science, engineering, and social science.

Eligibility: 2nd or 3rd year UG students with strong academic performance.

Stipend: CAD $6,000 + flight, accommodation, and insurance covered.

Visit Official Website

Charpak Scholarship

Description: Exchange or master's scholarship for Indian students to study in France.

Eligibility: UG/PG students enrolled in Indian institutions.

Stipend: €700/month + visa fee waiver + travel grant.

Visit Official Website

SN Bose Scholars Program

Description: Summer research program in the USA for Indian UG/PG students in science and engineering.

Eligibility: UG/PG students from recognized Indian institutions.

Stipend: Travel, health insurance, and living expenses fully covered.

Visit Official Website

Generation Google Scholarship (APAC)

Description: Scholarship for underrepresented students pursuing computer science degrees.

Eligibility: UG students with leadership and academic potential in tech.

Stipend: USD $1,000–$2,500 based on region and need.
